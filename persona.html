How Persona 5 Stole the Hearts of Millions

By Andy Nguyen

As the fifth installment of Atlus' coveted Persona series; Persona 5 has cemented itself as one of the most iconic titles in modern gaming. The classic is widely considered by many to be one of the best games ever made. I, myself, are one of those people.

Gameplay:

At its core Persona 5 is a JRPG, otherwise known as a Japanese Role-Playing Game. The main gameplay "loop" consists of dungeon crawling, interacting with a vibrant cast of characters, and a turn-based combat system that can be incredibly addicting. The main selling point of any game with a turn-based battle system is, well, their battes. So how does Persona 5's battles hold up? Pretty well, if I do say.
